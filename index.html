Welcome to Dice Game

Here are some rules:

  • Two players participate in rounds of the game.
  • Players can set goals in order to win the game. The game will automatically establish a default score of 50 points if users do not input a score. Players may, however, modify their goals whenever they choose.
  • A player may roll the dice as many times as he likes during each turn. His round score is increased by each outcome.
  • The player loses his full round score if he rolls a 1 on both dice. The following player then has a turn.
  • The player loses his full final score if he rolls a 6 on both dice. The following player then has a turn.
  • The option to "hold" allows the player to have his round score added to his overall score. The following player then has a turn.
